Malta
  • Only EU member state with full imputation system
  • Ideal tax residency status for individuals
  • Compliant with EU non-discrimination system
  • Gained approval from Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD)
  • Extensive network of double taxation treaties, plus benefits even when no bilateral treaty in effect
  • Refundable tax credit scheme – on revenues as dividends to shareholders, resident & non-resident
  • Corporation tax rate: 35% (30% tax refund for a non-resident shareholder)
  • Withholding tax : no witholding tax for non-resident shareholders or another company to which dividends are distributed
  • No anti-CFC rules or legislation are applicable in Malta
  • Support registration VAT number

Cyprus
  • Only EU member state with full imputation system
  • Cyprus has adopted International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS)
  • Lowest corporation tax in Europe of 12.5%
  • Favourable tax regime for Cyprus holding companies, including no tax on consolidation, no controlled foreign company (CFC) rules, Exemptions for Dividends received from abroad, Foreign PE trading profits & Profits from transactions in titles
  • No taxation on inward or outward dividends and no Capital Gain Tax (CGT) on sales of securities. Due to this many security traders have trading offices in Cyprus
  • There are more than 40 double tax treaties (DTT) concluded with other countries
  • Cyprus does not levy a WHT on dividends, interests, and royalties paid to non-residents of Cyprus
  • Except in the case of royalties earned on rights used within Cyprus, which are subject to WHT of 10% (5% in the case of cinematograph films)
  • Support registration VAT number

Switzerland

The Swiss taxation system is highly attractive for companies and private persons

  • Taxation on three levels ( Federal, Cantonal & Communal taxes )
  • Low corporate taxes (Flat rate 8.5% )
  • Low taxes for individuals
  • Case-by-case treatment in case of expatriates
  • Avoiding double taxation
  • Lowest value-added tax in Europe (regular VAT is 8%)
  • No inheritance taxes for immediate descendants
